How it works: The Investment Model

Purchase a herd:
Nyala or Sable

Minimum investment: R150 000
Maximum investment: R25 000 000

Purchasing your Animals

We can find the best deal and take care of all the logistics.
Whilst your animals are sedated for the relocation, we will take DNA samples, tag your animals, and embed microchips for lineage tracking.

1st year:
sell original herd

Recover the majority of your investment after the 1st generation of offspring has been born.
Or, keep your original herd for a larger ROI.

To Sell or no to sell?

Your choice.

But, keep in mind that selling your original herd results in a significant reduction in your total ROI.
See Profit calulation for details.

1st - 4th year:
Breeding

See a 5x growth in population over the duration of our agreement (5 years)

Breeding

Fertile females produce at least 1 offspring per year.
Assuming that 90% females are fertile, and that 50% of all offspring are female, your herd will grow by an order of 5x over the 5 year duration of the agreement.
See Profit calulation for details.

4th & 5th year:
Sell herd

Profits are shared according to a predefined agreement, after costs are deducted.

5 Year Contract

You actual ROI (after costs are deducted and profits have been shared) can be as high as 400% for Sables, and 160% for Nyala.
See Profit calulation for details.
