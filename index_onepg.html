Risk Protection

  • The South African Game trade is a mature and robust market, though it remains susceptible to various risks.
  • Inclusive in our offerring is animal insurance that reduces exposure to the risks of animal loss through predation, disease, and natural disasters. (poachers, are not a significant concern with these animals.)

Assuming that 90% females are fertile, and that 50% offspring are female, your herd will grow by an order of 5, over the 5 year duration of the agreement.
